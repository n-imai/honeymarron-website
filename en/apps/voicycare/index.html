VoicyCare

Music player app that amplifies volume up to 200% for earphones and headphones. Listen clearly to quiet music and podcasts. Ideal for people with hearing difficulties, elderly users, and anyone who listens to low-volume audio sources.

App Store Official Site

Key Features

  • Up to 200% volume boost
  • Music and podcast playback
  • Optimized for earphones/headphones
  • Simple and easy to use
  • Completely free

Why We Built This

"I want to help people with hearing difficulties enjoy audio more easily" — that's the idea behind VoicyCare.

As a developer, I sometimes struggle to hear quiet audio clearly. I searched for an app that could boost volume beyond 100%, but surprisingly, I couldn't find one that met my needs. So I decided to build it myself. I hope this app helps others who share the same challenge.
